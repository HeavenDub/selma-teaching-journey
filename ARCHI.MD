# GAME CONCEPT

Selma is an English teacher trainee studying at CRMEF in Safi.

Her dream is to become a certified English teacher and begin her professional career in Casablanca.

She starts her journey in Safi and travels north through Morocco.

Each city contains quests, lessons, challenges, mini-games, NPCs, teaching situations, and character growth opportunities.

The journey represents her transformation from nervous trainee to confident professional teacher.

The game should feel like:

* Halcyon Days
* Stardew Valley
* Coffee Talk
* Visual Novels
* JRPG World Maps

Combined into one wholesome educational RPG.
